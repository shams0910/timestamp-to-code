Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2024

This Privacy Policy describes how Timestamp to Code Converter ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our website.

Information We Collect

Usage Data

We may collect information about how you use our website, including which programming languages you select and how often you use the converter.

Analytics Data

We use Amplitude analytics to understand how users interact with our website. Amplitude may collect:

  • Page views and navigation patterns
  • Feature usage (which languages are selected most often)
  • Device and browser information
  • Session duration and frequency of visits
  • Geographic location (country/region level only)

Browser Storage

We use localStorage to remember your preferred programming languages for a better user experience. This data is stored locally in your browser and is not transmitted to our servers.

No Personal Information

We do not collect, store, or process any personal information such as names, email addresses, or specific IP addresses.

How We Use Information

  • To provide and maintain our timestamp conversion service
  • To remember your language preferences
  • To improve our website functionality based on usage patterns
  • To understand which features are most popular
  • To optimize user experience and performance

Third-Party Services

Amplitude Analytics

We use Amplitude to analyze website usage and improve our service. Amplitude's privacy policy can be found at https://amplitude.com/privacy.

Amplitude may use cookies and similar technologies to collect analytics data. You can opt out of Amplitude tracking by:

  • Using browser privacy settings to block analytics cookies
  • Using browser extensions that block tracking
  • Contacting us to request data deletion

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information. All data is stored locally in your browser and is not transmitted to our servers except for anonymous analytics data sent to Amplitude.

Data Retention

Analytics data is retained by Amplitude according to their data retention policies. Local storage data remains in your browser until you clear your browser data or we update our storage format.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Clear your browser's localStorage to remove saved preferences
  • Use our service without any data being stored
  • Opt out of analytics tracking through browser settings
  • Request deletion of your analytics data
  • Contact us with any privacy concerns

Children's Privacy

Our service does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

International Data Transfers

Analytics data may be processed by Amplitude in countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for such transfers.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or would like to request data deletion, please contact us through our website.
